About the Artist

Get to know Nourhan Haydar and her artistic journey

7+ Years

Nourhan Haydar

Visual artist specializing in contemporary paintings that blend traditional techniques with modern expression. Graduated from the Fine Arts Academy in 2016 with honors.

My work explores the intersection of nature, emotion, and abstract forms, creating visual narratives that invite viewers to explore their own interpretations.

I've participated in over 20 exhibitions locally and internationally, with works featured in private collections across three continents.
